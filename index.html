FAQ

Common Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?
+
We accommodate orders from as few as 10 pieces up to 10,000+. Our flexible system ensures the same premium quality at any scale, with competitive pricing for bulk orders.
How long does the process take from consultation to delivery?
+
Standard orders take 2–4 weeks. Rush orders can be delivered within 7–10 business days. We provide a clear timeline during the initial consultation so you can plan with confidence.
Do you offer fabric samples and prototypes before production?
+
Absolutely. We provide physical fabric swatches and produce a prototype garment for your approval before committing to full production — ensuring you're 100% satisfied before we scale.
Which Emirates do you deliver to?
+
We deliver across all 7 Emirates — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Fujairah, Ras Al Khaimah, and Umm Al Quwain. Collection from our facility or door-to-door delivery both available.
Can you match our exact brand colours and add our logo?
+
Yes. We use Pantone/CMYK colour matching and offer logo embroidery, screen printing, and heat transfer. Our team advises on the most durable method for your fabric and uniform type.
